üéØ Problem Statement

Stroke is one of the leading causes of death and long-term disability worldwide.
Early identification of individuals at high risk of stroke can help in taking preventive measures and improving patient outcomes.

However, predicting stroke risk is challenging because it depends on multiple medical and lifestyle factors such as age, blood pressure, glucose level, BMI, smoking habits, and existing health conditions.

The objective of this project is to:

Build a machine learning model that can predict the probability of stroke based on patient data

Identify the most important features contributing to stroke risk

Provide a reliable and scalable solution for early stroke risk prediction
